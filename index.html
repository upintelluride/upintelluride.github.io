Up in Telluride

Stories of Power, Secrets, and Survival in the Colorado High Country

At 9,000 feet, secrets have nowhere to hide

About the Author & Book

Up in Telluride by J. Michael Miller is a taut thriller set in the striking Colorado high country. Beneath Telluride's beauty lies a world of ambition, secrets, and betrayal. Miller, a longtime observer of Western life and politics, delivers a fast-paced story where wealth and power meet their reckoning at 9,000 feet.
